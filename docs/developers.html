Current OSCARS Developers

  • Chin Guok is the principal investigator and handles all aspects of network engineering.

  • David Robertson is the main developer of the reservation manager component and the Web interface. He writes portions of the scheduler having to do with router configuration within ESnet. He is also responsible for portions of the AAA component, for regression testing, release engineering, and portions of the OSCARS build process and documentation effort.

  • Vangelis Chaniotakis works on building the topology portion of the database for ESnet, on topology exchange and automated pathfinding issues, and on example clients and database utility scripts.

  • Mary Thompson handles security issues, including the use of signed SOAP messages using Rampart and Axis2, and the AAA component. She developed the original OSCARS WSDL schema. She also works on portions of the OSCARS build process and documentation effort, and on example clients.

  • Andrew Lake of Internet2 works on incorporating the DRAGON scheduler into OSCARS, incorporating the DICE control plane schema within OSCARS, developing one of the topology exchange managers (TEDB), and on example clients. He also works on major portions of the reservation manager including the choice of VLANS and the use of token exchange for layer 2 reservations.
